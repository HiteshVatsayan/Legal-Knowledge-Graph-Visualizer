# Legal-Knowledge-Graph-Visualizer


**Description:**
The Legal Knowledge Graph Visualizer is a web-based tool designed to visualize legal knowledge graphs (LKGs) for search queries. LKGs represent legal concepts and relationships between them, enabling the integration of data from diverse legal sources. This tool aims to provide users with a clear and intuitive interface to explore and understand legal concepts and their relationships.

**Key Features:**
1. **Concept Visualization:** The tool displays legal concepts as nodes and relationships between them as edges in a graph format, allowing users to visualize the structure of legal knowledge.
2. **Concept Centric Recursive Retrieval:** Users can perform concept-centric recursive retrieval to explore the implications and connections of legal concepts. For example, users can query who is affected by a specific client's actions and how.
3. **Semantic Annotation:** Legal concepts are semantically annotated to provide additional context and information, enhancing the understanding of legal relationships.
4. **Search Visualizations:** Search results are presented in a tree/folder structure, allowing users to navigate through related concepts and explore similar chunks of information.
5. **Semantic Vector Search:** The tool includes semantic vector search functionality, enabling users to find documents containing concepts related to their search terms for more comprehensive legal research.

**Purpose:**
The Legal Knowledge Graph Visualizer aims to facilitate legal research by providing users with an intuitive interface to explore and understand legal concepts and their relationships. By visualizing legal knowledge graphs and enabling concept-centric retrieval, the tool empowers users to conduct more effective and thorough legal research.

**Example Use Cases:**
1. **Exploring Rights under Copyright Law:** Users can visualize and explore the concepts related to rights under copyright law, understanding the relationships between different legal principles and outcomes.
2. **Tagging Concepts within Contract Law:** Users can optimize tagging within contract law by categorizing concepts such as "Patent Appeals" into specific areas of law, services, and forums/venues, enabling more granular and targeted queries.

**Target Audience:**
The Legal Knowledge Graph Visualizer is intended for legal professionals, researchers, students, and anyone interested in exploring legal concepts and relationships in a visual and intuitive manner.

**Future Enhancements:**
Future enhancements may include incorporating machine learning algorithms for automated concept tagging, integrating natural language processing techniques for improved semantic search, and expanding the scope of legal knowledge graphs to cover additional legal domains and jurisdictions.
